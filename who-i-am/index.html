Who I Am

I am Daniel M German, professor in the department of computer science at the University of Victoria. My duties are divided along two main areas:

  • Research. My main area of research is software engineering. In particular, software evolution, open source and intellectual property. In my spare time I do research in computational photography. A list of my papers can be found [here](http://turingmachine.org/~dmg/papers).
  • Teaching. I teach several courses at Uvic, primarily Database Systems (CSC370), Social and Professional Issues (SENG401), Media Applications (SENG410) and more recently Intro to Software Engineering (SENG265).
