This Is the Beginning of the World... Again

I am tired of web-based blogging. I want to blog when I want, where I want—that includes not having an internet connection—-and don’t be forced to use any specific infrastructure to do it.

Why? because I like my editor. And every time I have to use a different editing environment I feel trustrated.

So here it goes again… a reboot of my web site. Well, it just has to happen every few years…
